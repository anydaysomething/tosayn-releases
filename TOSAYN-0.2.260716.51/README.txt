TOSAYN — the only scanner app you need.
=========================================

Thank you for getting TOSAYN!


INSTALL
-------
1. Drag TOSAYN.app into your Applications folder.
2. Double-click it to start.


FIRST LAUNCH ON macOS ("Apple could not verify…")
-------------------------------------------------
macOS shows a security warning for apps downloaded outside the App Store.
This is normal and only happens once. To open TOSAYN:

1. Double-click TOSAYN.app. When the warning appears, click "Done"
   (NOT "Move to Trash").
2. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security.
3. Scroll down to the "Security" section. You will see:
   "TOSAYN.app was blocked to protect your Mac."
4. Click "Open Anyway" and confirm with your password or Touch ID.
5. One last dialog appears — click "Open Anyway" again.

That's it. From now on TOSAYN opens like any other app.

(On macOS 14 or older you can simply right-click TOSAYN.app and
choose "Open" instead.)


REQUIREMENTS
------------
- Apple Silicon Mac (M1 or later)
- macOS 15 Sequoia or newer
- A scanner: USB or network (AirPrint/eSCL). No drivers needed.


UPDATES
-------
TOSAYN keeps itself up to date. When a new version is available you
will be offered the update inside the app — one click, done. You can
also check manually via the TOSAYN menu → "Check for Updates…".


QUICK START
-----------
1. Turn your scanner on.
2. Click the big button. TOSAYN finds your scanner by itself.
3. Pick Document or Photo, put your page in, scan.
   The gear opens all settings; the folder icon shows your scans.
